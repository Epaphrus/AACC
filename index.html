Our Mission

At the Asia Africa Chamber of Commerce (AACC), our mission is to strengthen economic ties and enhance trade, industry, tourism, and investment (TITI) between Asia and Africa. We focus on collaboration and sustainable development, influencing policy at national and community levels. We support national chambers, foster private sector unity, and promote public-private partnerships. Our mission is achieved through well-developed proposals, stakeholder unity, and government collaboration to make Asia and Africa more attractive to investors.

Our Vision

The Asia Africa Chamber of Commerce envisions being a credible partner for governments, fostering a business environment that supports a robust private sector across Asia and Africa. We believe economic cooperation is key to shared prosperity. Our vision is to be a world-class reference point, enabling seamless trade and investment, and empowering women and youth. AACC aims to drive sustainable economic growth and serve as a trusted facilitator for trade and investment opportunities between Asia and Africa, bridging continents and building a legacy of collaborative success.
