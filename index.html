Welcome to Asia Africa Chamber of Commerce

Connecting businesses across two continents

Our main goal is to be a world class reference point, and organization that promotes and facilitates the TRADE, INDUSTRY, TOURISM and INVESTMENT (TITI) between Asia and Africa. ASIA AFRICA CHAMBER OF COMMERCE aims to translate economic co-operation into increased in disposable income with women and youth as priority end beneficiaries. Browse through our site to discover what we're all about.

Our Mission

Strengthen economic ties between Asia and Africa

Enhance trade, industry, tourism, and investment (TITI)

Foster collaboration and sustainable development

Support national chambers and promote public-private partnerships

Develop proposals and unite stakeholders

Make Asia and Africa more attractive to investors

Our Vision

Be a credible partner for governments

Foster a robust private sector across Asia and Africa

Enable seamless trade and investment

Empower women and youth

Drive sustainable economic growth

Bridge continents and build collaborative success

Our Journey and Goals

2020: Establishment

Asia Africa Chamber of Commerce founded to bridge economic ties between continents.

2021: Growing Membership

Expanded our network to include 500+ businesses across Asia and Africa.

2022: Economic Impact

Facilitated trade deals worth over $1 billion between member countries.

2023: Recognition

Awarded "Best International Trade Facilitator" by Global Business Association.

2025: Future Goals

Aim to double membership and facilitate $5 billion in annual trade between Asia and Africa.
