Our Journey and Goals

2020: Establishment

Asia Africa Chamber of Commerce founded to bridge economic ties between continents.

2021: Growing Membership

Expanded our network to include 500+ businesses across Asia and Africa.

2022: Economic Impact

Facilitated trade deals worth over $1 billion between member countries.

2023: Recognition

Awarded "Best International Trade Facilitator" by Global Business Association.

2025: Future Goals

Aim to double membership and facilitate $5 billion in annual trade between Asia and Africa.
