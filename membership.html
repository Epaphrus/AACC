Terms & Conditions
1. Membership Eligibility

Membership in the AACC is open to businesses, organizations, and individuals operating in Asia and Africa or having business interests in these regions. The AACC reserves the right to reject or terminate membership applications at its discretion.

2. Membership Benefits

As a member of the AACC, you will have access to networking events, workshops, seminars, and other opportunities aimed at fostering business growth and development. You will receive updates on market trends, economic insights, and industry-specific information to aid your business decision-making process. The AACC may provide special offers, discounts, or promotional opportunities for members on products and services from selected partners.

3. Membership Fees

Membership fees are payable annually or as per the agreed terms during the application process. The AACC reserves the right to revise membership fees with prior notice to existing members.

4. Member Conduct

Members are expected to maintain professionalism, honesty, and integrity while representing the AACC or participating in its events and activities. Discrimination, harassment, or any form of unethical behavior will not be tolerated and may lead to membership termination.

5. Intellectual Property

The AACC retains all rights to its intellectual property, including but not limited to its name, logo, website content, and publications. Members must not use the AACC's intellectual property without explicit written permission.

6. Privacy and Data Protection

The AACC will collect and process personal information in accordance with its privacy policy. By joining as a member, you consent to such data processing. Members are responsible for ensuring the accuracy and confidentiality of their personal information provided to the AACC.

7. Termination of Membership

7.1. Cancellation of membership has to be made in writing latest 30 days before the end of a contribution year. Unless such notice of intention is given, any subscription is payable in full latest two weeks after the beginning of the next contribution year. The member is not entitled to any refund, in full or part, of any annual subscription paid.

7.2. The AACC shall not be liable for any direct, indirect, or consequential damages arising from your membership or use of its services.

7.3. The AACC does not guarantee the accuracy, completeness, or reliability of information provided by members or third parties.

8. Governing Law

These terms and conditions shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Kenya. Any disputes arising from or related to these terms and conditions shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Kenya.
